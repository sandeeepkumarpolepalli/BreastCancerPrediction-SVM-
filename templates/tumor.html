Benign Tumors

Benign tumors are non-cancerous and typically harmless. In many cases, doctors may choose to monitor the lump rather than remove it. However, if you experience discomfort or irritation, consult your doctor.

Malignant Tumors

Malignant tumors are cancerous and can aggressively invade nearby tissues. If a tumor is suspected to be malignant, a biopsy is often performed by the doctor to assess its severity and determine the best course of treatment.
